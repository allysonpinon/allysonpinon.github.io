Love Letter: Rolling Stones


This is the content from my Love Letter Presentation on the Rolling Stones. They have been one of my favorite bands since I was born, as they were my mom's favorite band as well. They are one of the most well known bands ever and have been together for over 50 years. They also have branded themselves amazingly and have one one of the most recognizable logos in the world.
